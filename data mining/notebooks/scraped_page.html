          Virtual Assistant, are you there?

          Hey there! We're looking for a Virtual Assistant to provide comprehensive administrative and sales support to a Sales Head of a company focused on delivering end-to-end insect farming service. If you are highly organized and proactive with excellent German and English communication skills, keep reading!

          100% Remote

          Salary in USD

          LATAM/Europe - Reporting to Vienna,Austria, [Central European Time (CET]

          Full Time

          Advanced English

          About Our Client

          You will be working for our client, a company that has launched several insect technology products on the market. Their Hive PRO Insect Factory is a turn-key solution for the food, feed, and recycling industries. Seeded with Seedlings (baby larvae), Hive PROs turn industrial quantities of organic wastes into insect products for the feed and agricultural market.

          About Athyna

          Athyna is a Global Talent Platform, helping align world-class talent with the best employment opportunities across the globe.

          We believe in the future of work. That’s why we are a 100% globally distributed team across 5 continents. We believe in people, and we value freedom, integrity, quality, and sustainability.

          We’re proudly carbon neutral as an organization and 5% of all revenue funds impact-driven startups & climate tech. We’re very keen on social impact - we truly care about everyone on our team, and we want to make the world a better place for all of us.

          In your day-to-day, you will
          • Serve as a primary point of contact for clients and colleagues, effectively communicating in both German and English.
          • Handle email correspondence, compose professional messages, and respond to inquiries promptly and accurately.
          • Manage the Sales Head's calendar, schedule appointments, and coordinate meetings, ensuring all relevant parties are informed.
          • Prepare and format sales reports, presentations, and proposals.
          • Organize and maintain sales-related documentation, including contracts, agreements, and client records.
          • Collaborate with the Sales Head to prepare sales forecasts, track sales performance, and provide regular reports and analysis.
          • Conduct follow-ups with clients to nurture relationships and identify opportunities for upselling or cross-selling.
          • Arrange travel itineraries, accommodations, and transportation for the Sales Head's business trips.
          • Assist with event planning and logistics for sales-related conferences, exhibitions, or client meetings.
          Skills you have
          • Fluent in German and English, with exceptional verbal and written communication skills in both languages.
          • Proven experience as a virtual assistant, administrative assistant, or similar role, preferably in a sales or international business setting.
          • Strong organizational and time management skills, with the ability to prioritize tasks and meet deadlines.
          • Proficiency in using productivity tools such as Microsoft Office Suite, CRM systems, and project management platforms.
          • Excellent attention to detail and accuracy in data entry, documentation, and correspondence.
          • Strong research skills, with the ability to gather and analyze information effectively.
          • Ability to work independently and remotely, demonstrating self-motivation and initiative.

          • Experience as Sales VA
